REVIEW

Based on the beloved novel, Bridge to Terabithia it shows us the friendship between Jesse and Leslie feeling lived-in and awkward in all the right ways, and the film’s make believe kingdom Terabithia works best as a reflection of their inner worlds rather than a CGI showcase. The fantasy elements enhance rather than replace the story’s emotional message.

Josh Hutcherson conveys Jesse’s quiet longing and fear, while AnnaSophia Robb brings luminous warmth, daring, and emotional pain to Leslie. The film also respects tougher themes—grief, faith, and growth with a gentleness that never talks down to its audience. As a coming-of-age story about loss and the power of imagination, Bridge to Terabithia is honest, tender, painful, and deeply affecting.
